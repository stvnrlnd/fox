A bit about me

Hey,

My name is Steven. I'm passionate about education, technology, environmental awareness, art, eco-friendly solutions to everything, yoga, Sci-Fi shows, super heroes, and bow ties. Web development is my one true love, though.

Tallahassee, Florida is where I call home, but I live on the internet, so it's always pretty easy to reach me. When I'm not entrenched in code or teaching myself something new, I'm hanging out on Twitter or Facebook. Feel free to drop in and say hello. I love to meet new people, especially budding entrepreneurs and creative professionals. :)

My mom is cool. She taught me that people are the most important part of life. And the people that I've had the pleasure to meet have certainly made me a better person.

I want to work with a SaaS startup where I can truly apply myself and build some really kick-ass things. I'm not just a developer, I'm an end-user, I'm a listener, and I'm a dreamer of dreams. Please let me know if you think that I'd be a great fit for your team!
